## Overview

"20CC" is the **proprietary SID replay routine of the Dutch group 20CC (20th
Century Composers)**, developed by **Falco Paul** (co-founder with Edwin van
Santen), from ~late 1986. It was never a released tool — it was the group's
in-house player tech, embedded in their compositions and lent to collaborators
like Reyn Ouwehand. 242 files here across 30 composers.

## Quirks & gotchas

See the `quirks` array. Load-bearing: it's a **group-internal routine, not a
public editor**; a key **CSDb correction** (the cited "20CC Music Editor" release
is a third-party Future-Composer-derived wrapper, not the real routine); and a
**founding-date conflict** (late-1986 primary source vs a mid-1990s secondary
